Lifestyle products inspired by Karnataka's art and craft — curtains, cushions and table linen carrying real, named heritage techniques.

What we are

Chiguru means "sprout" or new bud in Kannada — a nod to growth, heritage coming alive, and new beginnings. It's a home lifestyle brand rooted in the living traditions of Karnataka, bringing the state's most treasured art forms — from the lacquered toys of Channapatna to the silver inlay of Bidriware — into your home.

The series

One collection. One art form. One story at a time. Our first five: the bright lacquerwork of Channapatna, the centuries-old silver inlay of Bidriware from Bidar, the hand-painted circular cards of Ganjifa, the mirror-work embroidery of Lambani, and the raw folk murals of Chitra — each one brought into your home exactly as it deserves to be.

How we're built

Every Chiguru product is made in close collaboration with the artisan communities behind each art form. They're not suppliers — they're the reason any of this exists.
